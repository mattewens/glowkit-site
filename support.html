Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → GlowKit → Cancel.

Where is my data stored?
All data is stored on your device only. We don't have servers or accounts.

I lost my streak data
If you deleted and reinstalled the app, your data cannot be recovered as it was stored locally on your device.

How do I restore my purchase?
Open GlowKit → Settings → Restore Purchase.
