Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

GlowKit ("the App") is a daily routine tracking app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Stored on Your Device Only

  • Your routine habits and preferences
  • Daily completion history
  • Streak and Glow Score data
  • Notification preferences
  • Display preferences (theme, accent colour)

This data never leaves your device. GlowKit does not have a server, database, or user accounts. All your data is stored locally using your device's built-in storage.

Data Collected by Third Parties

If you subscribe to GlowKit Premium, your payment is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions.

We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat may collect anonymised purchase data. RevenueCat privacy policy.

Analytics

GlowKit does not collect analytics, usage data, or personal information. We do not track you.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any personal data to third parties. Because we don't collect any.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting the app. This permanently removes all your routine data, streaks, and preferences.

Children's Privacy

GlowKit does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
